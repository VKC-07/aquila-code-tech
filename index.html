FAQ

Questions institutions usually ask before taking the first meeting.

Who is Aquila designed to work with?

Aquila Code Tech is designed for colleges, departments, CRT coordinators, and placement teams that need more structured campus recruitment preparation for final-year batches.

Can the engagement be customized for specific batches or departments?

Yes. The structure can be adjusted around academic timelines, current batch readiness, delivery preference, and the type of companies students are preparing for.

What preparation areas are typically included?

Common coverage includes aptitude, reasoning, coding, communication, mock interviews, group discussions, resume basics, and company-specific final preparation.

Can Aquila support pre-drive preparation instead of a long CRT cycle?

Yes. Aquila can also operate as a shorter targeted intervention when an institution needs support closer to placement drives rather than a long-format engagement.

How should an institution start the conversation?

Share the institution name, batch size, preferred delivery mode, current CRT stage, and the expected placement window. That is enough to start a useful discussion.
